FIRST GRADING LESSONS

Foundation concepts in media and communication literacy - building essential digital citizenship skills

1

Media and Communication

  • Shannon-Weaver Model: Known as the mother of all communication models
  • Elements: Information source, Transmitter, Channel, Receiver, Destination
  • Components: Sender, Encoder, Decoder, Receiver, Noise, Feedback
  • Foundation for understanding how information flows between parties
2

Technology Literacy

  • Ability to use digital technology, communication tools, and networks
  • Skills: Locate, evaluate, use, and create information
  • Etymology: From Greek 'techne' (art/skill/craft) + 'logos' (word/reason/discourse)
  • Systematic study and application of techniques and tools
3

Responsible Use of Media and Information

  • Flame War: Series of angry, disparaging comments in ongoing online arguments
  • Importance of respectful digital communication
  • Understanding netiquette and online behavior
  • Consequences of irresponsible media use
4

Evolution from Traditional to New Media

  • Electronic Age: Began with electronic equipment including computers
  • Transition from print to digital media platforms
  • Evolution of communication technologies
  • Impact on information dissemination and access
5

Media and Information Sources

  • Newspaper: Printed/distributed daily or weekly with news, opinions, features, advertising
  • Magazine: Printed or digital collection of texts (essays, articles, stories, poems) at regular intervals
  • Media: Means of communication that reach or influence people widely
  • Understanding different formats and their purposes
6

Media and Information Sources (Technical)

  • AVR Section: Provides audio-visual support services
  • Facilitates teaching and learning processes
  • Technical infrastructure for media delivery
  • Program structure and instruction systems

SECOND GRADING LESSONS

Advanced media literacy and interactive digital content creation - mastering multimedia technologies

1

Opportunities and Challenges in Media

  • Cybercrime: Criminal activity involving internet, computer systems, or technology
  • Threats: Hacking, identity theft, data breaches
  • Digital safety and security awareness
  • Balancing opportunities with risk management
2

Blended Learning & Current Trends

  • Blended Learning: Combination of classroom learning and online learning
  • Platforms: Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Cisco Webex
  • Style of education via electronic and online media
  • Future trends in media and information delivery
3

Media and Information Literate Individual

  • Improved learning environments through digital sources
  • Popular platforms: Yahoo, Wikipedia, Facebook, Google, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube
  • Making learning a choice through accessible information
  • Top websites for information and knowledge sharing
4

Text Information and Media

  • Emphasis: Importance or value given to text-based content
  • Typography: Design and arrangement of text
  • Elements: Fonts, spacing, alignment, color, creativity
  • Text as fundamental medium for information delivery
5

Visual Media and Information

  • Design Elements: Line, Shape, Value
  • Visual communication principles
  • Creating effective visual messages
  • Understanding visual literacy
6

Audio Information and Media

  • Radio Broadcast: Live or recorded audio sent through waves
  • Reaches wide audiences effectively
  • Audio as information delivery medium
  • Sound-based communication channels
7

Motion Media and Information

  • Motion Media: Visual media that gives appearance of movement
  • Video content and animation
  • Dynamic storytelling through motion
  • Engagement through moving images
8

Manipulative/Interactive Media

  • Media Platform: Service or method that delivers media to audiences
  • Examples: TikTok, Snapchat, Reddit, YouTube, Digg
  • Interactive and participatory content
  • User-generated and social media
9

Multimedia Information and Media

  • Static Media: Content that does not change
  • Dynamic Media: Content constantly updated and interactive
  • Digital advertising channels (60% audience preference)
  • Categories: Display ads, social media ads, retargeting, video ads
10

Dimensions of Media

  • Visual + Audio: Technology combining sound and visuals
  • Audiovisual technology for information presentation
  • Creating immersive experiences
  • Multi-sensory media delivery
11

Evaluating and Creating Multimedia

  • Digital Library: Special library with focused collection of digital objects
  • Producing creative media-based presentations
  • Multimedia evaluation criteria
  • Creating professional digital content
